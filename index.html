TOWER I

3-BR & 4-BR

Tower I presents 3- and 4-bedroom apartments with private lift lobby reserved for the most discerning individuals. The apartments feature a double-height ceiling in the living dining area together with spacious balconies, providing unparalleled views of the natural environment and city beyond. Offering the luxury of complete privacy, Tower I is further enhanced with a selection of exclusive amenities for its residents, including a private clubhouse equipped with an exquisite indoor pool and gym.



TOWER II & III

2-BR & 3-BR
